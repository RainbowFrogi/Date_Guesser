How to Play

Welcome to Guess The Date! Here's how to play:

The game presents you with a picture of a random location from the years 1800 to 2024. Your task is to guess the year by adjusting the year slider and identify the country where the picture was taken. The closer your year guess is to the correct date, the more points you earn.
